The Complete Daily Report,
From Wake to Sleep.

ChronoFit aims to build a healthy day with optimal energy balance by meticulously capturing your nutrition, hydration, body metrics (weight, muscle mass, body fat, blood sugar), and workouts.

  • Health Connect Auto-Sync: Effortlessly sync sleep, blood pressure, and body composition data from other apps.
  • Precise Barcode Logging: Log exact macros and ingredients instantly with a single scan, eliminating manual errors.
  • HIIT & AI Analysis: Elevate your training with smartwatch integration and comprehensive AI evaluations.

Time-Series Energy Balance &
Intuitive Animations.

Track your workouts, meals, and hydration in chronological order from the moment you wake up.
We analyze your real-time surplus or deficit of calories, carbs, protein, fat, water, and sodium against your BMR and specific dietary goals.
Let our intuitive animations guide you to finish your day perfectly balanced.

In-Depth Analysis by Your
Hyper-Personalized AI Coach.

Go far beyond simple summaries. Receive an expert-level 'Metabolic Score' evaluating your macro balance, blood sugar stability, and fat-burning efficiency.
Experience close-contact feedback tailored to the exact time of day—whether it's an action guide for the remaining hours or a predictive analysis of your body's changes for tomorrow.

Accurate Food Logging
via Barcode Scan.

Escape the inaccuracies of manual entry or visual camera estimates.
Scan a barcode once to accurately log highly detailed nutritional facts and raw ingredients.
True health management begins with data integrity.

Precision Food Analysis &
Custom Nutrition Simulation.

Evaluate the quality of your food comprehensively using Nutri-Score and detailed ingredient breakdowns.
Before consuming, simulate exactly how a product will impact your specific dietary targets (e.g., a high-protein goal).
We guide your optimal intake through systematic comparisons within the same food category.

Multi-Barcode Scan &
Live Product Comparison.

Stop hesitating between similar products.
Scan multiple barcodes consecutively to compare core nutritional facts side-by-side on a single, intuitive screen.
Make fast, data-driven decisions instantly to choose the food that perfectly aligns with your needs.

Live Training Guide
via Dedicated Wear OS App.

Install the ChronoFit app on your Wear OS smartwatch (e.g., Galaxy Watch) to seamlessly sync and record your heart rate, burned calories, and workout intensity in real-time.
Follow intuitive, on-wrist training guides perfectly synchronized with your personalized HIIT routines.

Smart Band &
Non-Wear OS Support.

Galaxy Fit and other fitness tracker users can still enjoy ChronoFit's professional interval training features.
Use our in-app timer, and your device's data will be seamlessly integrated via Health Connect (Data Fusion).
Your heart rate and movement data are automatically segmented by your interval patterns for deep AI analysis.

Workout Visualization &
Social Media Sharing.

Capture your successful training results in a single, shareable image.
Add intuitive overlay stickers containing your core fitness data—like duration, calories burned, and heart rate zones—directly to your photos.
Share effortlessly to stay motivated and inspire others on your journey.

Data Overlay Extraction
for Fitness Creators (PRO).

Dramatically reduce complex and tedious video editing time.
Focus entirely on recording your workout with ChronoFit. Post-workout, easily extract video clips visualizing your live timer, session info, real-time heart rate, and calorie burn.
Simply drop these overlay clips directly into your editing timeline to finalize professional-grade fitness content.

Live Video Sync &
A-B Loop Guides.

Link YouTube fitness videos directly into the app to train while watching.
Set specific segments to loop repeatedly, ensuring you perfectly master complex movements.
Maintain correct posture and pacing by observing the experts in real-time, maximizing your training efficiency.

User-Curated Videos &
Creator Promotion.

Search YouTube or enter links directly to register your favorite workout videos into the ChronoFit ecosystem.
Fitness creators can register their own content to naturally promote their channels to an active user base.
Only the top 50 proven videos, curated through community ratings (likes/dislikes), are showcased to highlight high-quality content.

Exercise Database &
Data-Driven Analysis.

From bodyweight routines to kettlebells and gym machines, we've systematically built a comprehensive database of essential exercises and targeted muscle stimulation data.
Experience an objective, sophisticated evaluation of your workout logs based on our continuously expanding fitness library.

Interval Segmentation &
Comprehensive Charts.

We precisely segment your heart rate, distance, and step data for every single set of your interval training.
Grasp your physical capabilities and stamina throughout the entire workout flow via detailed, segmented charts, enabling you to build highly systematic training plans.

AI Workout Analysis
Powered by Sensor Data.

Log the fine details of your workout: exercise type, targeted muscles, weight, reps, and specific training frameworks like EMOM or AMRAP.
By combining these configurations with live sensor data from your smart devices, our AI delivers an objective and comprehensive diagnostic report of your physical performance.
