[Supsiki] Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Month] [Day], 2025

ChronoLab (hereinafter 'Company') values users' privacy and complies with Google Play Policies. We do not transmit sensitive health information to our servers or store it separately.

Article 1 (Purpose of Processing)

  • Service Usage: Google Sign-In (Authentication only).
  • Health Service: Analysis using on-device data and Health Connect.
  • Community: Sharing workout routines (with non-identifiable IDs).
  • Advertising: AdMob personalized ads (for free users).

Article 2 (Age Restriction)

This service is for adults aged 18+. Data of users under 18 will be deleted upon discovery.

Article 3 (Data Collection & Processing)

  1. On-Device Only (Not sent to Server): Sleep, Nutrition, Weight, Heart Rate, etc. are processed/stored ONLY on the user's device.
  2. Limited Server Transmission: Workout routine data, Nickname, Firestore Non-identifiable ID (only when sharing or reporting errors).
  3. Auto-Collected: Logs, Device Info, ADID (Statistical use only).

Article 4 (Health Connect Data)

Data obtained from Health Connect is used solely for providing app features and is not transmitted to Company servers or shared with third parties.

Article 7 (Data Delegation)

We use the following services for operation:

  • Google LLC: Firebase (Auth/DB), Analytics
  • Google Play: Billing & Subscription
  • Google AdMob: Advertising

Article 10 (Personalized Ads)

AdMob may collect ADID. You can opt-out via device settings (Settings > Security & Privacy > Ads > Delete Advertising ID).

Article 12 (Data Protection Officer)
