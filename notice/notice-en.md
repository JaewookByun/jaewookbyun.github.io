# ChronoFit v1.1 Update

Greetings from ChronoLab. The primary focus of this update is our official app rebranding.

---

## 1. App Rebranding: From Supsiki to ChronoFit

Our app's official name has been changed from Supsiki to ChronoFit. This decision was made to clarify our direction and establish our identity as a comprehensive fitness platform, evolving beyond simple diet management. 

While the previous name may still appear in certain areas of the system, we will sequentially update and resolve this in upcoming releases.

## 2. In-Workout Video Streaming & Custom Video Configuration

You can now watch instructional videos while training. 

Using the in-app or YouTube search functions, users can preset a tutorial video of their choice for each specific exercise. This allows you to reference the video guide in real-time during your workout, ensuring accurate posture and execution.

![In-Workout Video Screen 2](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JaewookByun/jaewookbyun.github.io/main/notice/1.1/2.jpg)

## 3. Video Registration Support for Fitness Creators (18 Languages Supported)

ChronoFit now provides a feature that allows fitness creators to register their own YouTube videos as the representative tutorial for specific exercises. 

ChronoFit currently supports 18 languages globally. As our service expands and the global user base grows, registered tutorial videos will naturally gain exposure to users across various countries. We anticipate this will make a long-term, positive contribution to increasing your channel views and securing a global subscriber base.
